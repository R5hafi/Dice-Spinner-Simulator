Octahedron dice and Spinner Game

By: Ryan Shafi

How to Play

Materials

In order to play this game the player must make use of the 8-sided dice simulator and the 4-sectioned spinner provided on this website to achieve a total sum.

Rules and Prizes

It costs 5 virtual school dollars to play 1 round, which is one click on "roll and spin".
If you roll a total sum of 2 or 12 you win $20. If you roll a sum of 3, 4, 10, or 11 you get your money back.
Any other sum (5, 6, 7, 8 or 9) results in a loss of $5.
